# Criminal Roleplay

Criminal roleplay should be story-driven, realistic, and enjoyable for everyone involved.

## Criminal RP Expectations

- Build escalation before violence.
- Give other players a fair chance to respond.
- Do not treat crime as a money grind only.
- Follow robbery limits, scene limits, and cop minimums.
- Do not camp illicit areas or abuse mechanics.

## Conflict Roleplay

Conflict should create stories, not frustration. Winning should never be more important than roleplay quality.
