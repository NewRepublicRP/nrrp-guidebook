# Civilian Roleplay

Civilian roleplay is the foundation of the city. Civilians create everyday interactions, businesses, jobs, stories, and conflicts that make New Republic RP feel alive.

## Civilian Expectations

- Create believable characters.
- Build stories over time.
- Use legal jobs and businesses to create roleplay.
- Interact with other players naturally.
- Avoid unrealistic behavior designed only to provoke police or staff.

## Business Roleplay

Business owners are expected to operate professionally, create roleplay opportunities, and follow any business rules or licensing requirements established by staff.
