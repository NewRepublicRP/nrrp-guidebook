# Emergency Roleplay

Emergency roleplay includes law enforcement, fire, EMS, dispatch, DOT, and other public safety scenes.

## Expectations

- Maintain professionalism in character.
- Use radio traffic appropriately.
- Respect scene command and active incident structure.
- Allow medical and investigative roleplay to develop.
- Do not rush scenes for convenience.

## EMS and Fire Scenes

When EMS or fire personnel are available, players should allow them to perform medical roleplay before respawning or leaving the scene.
