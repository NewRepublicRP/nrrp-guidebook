# Scene Etiquette

Good scene etiquette helps everyone enjoy roleplay.

## During a Scene

- Stay in character.
- Do not interrupt roleplay with OOC arguments.
- Allow others time to speak and react.
- Avoid talking over people on radio or voice.
- Roleplay injuries, fear, stress, and consequences realistically.

## After a Scene

If something went wrong, finish the scene first when possible. Then open a ticket or report through the proper channels.
