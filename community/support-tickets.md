# Support Tickets

Support tickets are the proper way to contact staff about rule issues, technical problems, applications, appeals, and private concerns.

## When to Open a Ticket

- Rule questions
- Player reports
- Staff complaints
- Ban appeals
- Technical issues
- Refund requests
- Department concerns

## Ticket Expectations

Provide clear details, screenshots, clips, timestamps, and names when available. Do not falsify or edit evidence in a misleading way.
