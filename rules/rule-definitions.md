# Roleplay Rule Definitions

## FailRP

FailRP is any action that breaks realism, avoids roleplay, disrupts a scene, or uses game mechanics in a way that harms the roleplay experience.

## RDM

Random Deathmatch is attacking or killing another player without proper roleplay, escalation, intent, or reason.

## VDM

Vehicle Deathmatch is using a vehicle to attack, injure, or kill another player without proper roleplay or escalation.

## Metagaming

Metagaming is using out-of-character information to influence in-character actions.

## Powergaming

Powergaming is forcing outcomes, using unrealistic mechanics, or giving yourself an unfair advantage through actions that do not allow other players reasonable roleplay options.

## Fear RP

Fear RP means valuing your character's life and reacting realistically to danger.
