# Server Rules

## 1. Going Out of Character

Roleplay before out-of-character discussion.

Players should not go out of character during active roleplay. If you have an issue with how a scene is going, finish the scene and open a ticket in Discord afterward.

Do not use OOC chat or out-of-character comments to end a scene because it is not going your way. Doing so may be considered FailRP.

## 2. Deathmatching

Killing or attacking another player with little to no roleplay is strictly prohibited.

This includes RDM, VDM, starting fights for no valid roleplay reason, and attacking others without proper interaction or escalation.

If a traffic accident occurs and someone is hit by a vehicle, the scene must be roleplayed with EMS and police when appropriate.

## 3. Downed and New Life Rule

If you are downed and removed from a scene by EMS, fire, police, or another player, you may not return to that scene until the situation is fully complete.

This rule applies to civilians, law enforcement, EMS, and fire personnel.

If you are taken to the hospital and cannot check in, you are still required to roleplay the hospital admission. EMS should be used when available. Skipping EMS or abusing revive mechanics may be considered FailRP.

You may not use Discord, OOC chat, or other external communication to request pickup or rescue. Using `/911` is the proper in-character method.

Using respawn mechanics while in police custody or EMS care to avoid roleplay is considered FailRP.

## 4. Powergaming

Powergaming is using game mechanics, external information, unrealistic roleplay, or forced actions to give your character an unfair advantage.

Players must allow others a fair chance to roleplay and respond to situations.

## 5. Metagaming

Metagaming is using out-of-character information to influence in-character decisions.

Examples include stream sniping, using information from livestreams, using Discord or other third-party applications during roleplay, using information from another character, or acting on information your character did not learn in roleplay.

## 6. Offensive RP, Suicide RP, and Combat Logging

Roleplay involving sexual assault, rape, non-consensual touching, or similar conduct is strictly prohibited and may result in an immediate ban.

Suicide RP is not allowed.

Combat logging, disconnecting, forcibly respawning, or leaving the server to avoid consequences or active roleplay is prohibited.

## 7. Explicit Conduct

Racial slurs, sexual harassment, sexist remarks, derogatory comments, transphobic or homophobic language, and other hateful conduct are not allowed.

Inappropriate character names, including names involving sexual innuendo or sexually suggestive content, are also prohibited.

## 8. Exploiting Bugs

Do not exploit bugs, glitches, or broken mechanics for personal gain.

If you discover a bug, you are required to report it. Failure to report an exploit may result in item seizure, money seizure, suspension, or ban.

## 9. Interactions Between Your Characters

Your characters do not know each other unless approved through roleplay by staff.

You may not transfer money, items, vehicles, property, or any other assets between your own characters. You may not use a friend or third party to transfer assets between characters.

## 10. Robbing Players

When robbing another player, you may only take what they currently have on their person or in their inventory.

You may not force a player to transfer bank funds, sell vehicles, give property, empty apartments or storage, or transfer assets outside of the immediate roleplay scene.

If robbing or kidnapping a player, you may not kill them unless they refuse to comply, resist, or escalate the situation through roleplay.

## 11. Exploiting Scripts or GTA V Mechanics

Knowingly abusing scripts, game physics, or GTA V mechanics to gain an advantage is not allowed.

This includes using unrealistic physics, broken mechanics, or unintended script behavior to benefit yourself or harm others.

## 12. Failure to Roleplay and Unrealistic RP

New Republic RP is a realistic roleplay server. You are expected to remain in character and roleplay at all times.

If rules are broken during a scene, continue the roleplay and report the issue afterward.

Abusing in-game mechanics, physics, or unrealistic actions to gain an advantage may result in punishment.

## 13. AFK and Money Farming

Avoiding roleplay to farm money is considered FailRP.

If another player approaches you for a roleplay scenario, you may not ignore the situation simply to continue farming, grinding, or making money.

## 14. Safe Zones

Safe zones are crime-free areas. Violence, robbery, drug sales, kidnapping, and starting criminal activity are prohibited in safe zones.

If you are fleeing from a crime and enter a safe zone, you are not automatically protected.

## 15. Impersonating Emergency Personnel

Civilians may not impersonate emergency personnel or whitelisted departments, including police, fire, EMS, DOT, or any whitelisted emergency or government agency.

## 16. Stealing or Harming Protected Vehicles or Personnel

Civilians may not steal EMS, fire, or DOT vehicles.

Kidnapping, killing, robbing, or holding EMS/fire personnel hostage is prohibited unless otherwise approved by staff for a specific event or storyline.

Hospitals and fire stations are protected areas.

## 17. Robbery Rules

Robbers must have a backpack, bag, or similar item visible while conducting a robbery to show where stolen items are being placed.

Negotiations may be conducted in person, by radio, or by phone between police and robbers.

## 18. Cop Baiting

Cop baiting is prohibited.

Do not intentionally attempt to get police attention by doing unrealistic, unnecessary, or disruptive actions that your character would not reasonably do near law enforcement.

## 19. EMS Authority and Medical Custody

Once revived or treated by EMS, you are in medical custody until released by medical personnel.

You must roleplay your injuries for at least 10 minutes after being discharged or cleared, unless EMS advises otherwise through roleplay.

## 20. Cop Minimum for Kidnapping

To kidnap a civilian, there must be at least 2 officers on duty.

To kidnap or take an officer hostage, there must be at least 7 officers on duty.

## 21. Police Mistreatment and Appeals

If you believe you were mistreated during a police situation, do not argue or attempt to police the police during the scene.

Use the proper channels after the scene, such as speaking with a supervisor, filing an agency complaint, or opening a support ticket.

## 22. RDM and VDM

RDM, or Random Deathmatch, is killing or attempting to kill another player without proper roleplay, intent, or escalation.

VDM, or Vehicle Deathmatch, is using a vehicle to kill or attempt to kill another player without proper roleplay, intent, or escalation.

Deadly force and violent actions must be supported by roleplay.

## 23. Fear RP

Fear RP means acting as your character realistically would when faced with danger.

For example, if you are alone and multiple armed individuals are holding you at gunpoint, you are expected to value your life and comply unless there is a realistic opportunity to do otherwise.

## 24. Perma-Death and Death Penalty

The administration team may issue a character death penalty or require perma-death if a character becomes a repeat offender of deadly crimes, shows blatant toxicity, or creates harmful roleplay patterns.

## 25. Vigilante, Hitman, and Sniping RP

Vigilante, hitman, and unrealistic assassin-style roleplay are not allowed.

Your character is a normal person and should be roleplayed as such. You are not a superhero, action movie assassin, or one-person army.

## 26. Server Restart Rules

A minimum of 10 minutes after an official server restart is required before committing crimes.

All criminal activity must stop 30 minutes prior to an official server restart.

## 27. Scene Limits

The maximum number of civilians involved in a criminal scene is 5 civilians.

Gangs may have up to 7 members outside of their territory and no set limit within their own territory, unless staff determines the scene is excessive or disruptive.

## 28. Torture RP

Torture RP is not allowed on New Republic RP.

## 29. Camping Illicit Areas

Camping illicit locations is prohibited.

This includes drug collection areas, processing locations, illegal job areas, or similar locations.

Police may not camp drug processing or collection areas solely to stop and arrest players leaving the area.

## 30. False Flag Operations

Civilians who impersonate a gang, group, or organization for the purpose of creating conflict may be subject to severe in-character consequences, including perma-death from the group they are impersonating, if approved by staff.

## 31. Body Cameras

Body cameras may only be used by law enforcement officers.

Civilians may only use recordings in roleplay if their phone is visibly out and recording video during the scene.

## 32. Radio Rules

Using a radio when your character would not realistically be able to is considered FailRP.

This includes using a radio while cuffed, zip-tied, downed, unconscious, actively restrained, or in a situation where radio use would be physically impossible.
