# General Rules

## 1. Communication

You must be able to understand, speak, and type English, as New Republic RP is a North American-based server.

You must have a working microphone. New Republic RP is an inclusive community that welcomes players from all backgrounds, including those with disabilities. If you have a disability or limitation that prevents you from using a microphone, reach out to staff so another method of communication can be reviewed.

## 2. Respect for the Community

Be respectful to all players, staff members, administrators, and community members. Your actions in-game should not interfere with another player's ability to enjoy the server.

Do not send angry, hostile, or offensive out-of-character messages because of in-game events. Do not argue with staff over a ruling during a scene. If you disagree with a decision, open a ticket and handle the matter privately through the proper channels.

Trolling players, staff, police, EMS, fire, civilians, or any community member is not allowed.

## 3. Administrator Instructions and Rulings

Staff and administrator instructions are final during active situations. If you believe a ruling was made in error, the issue must be handled outside the game through Discord by creating a support ticket.

Do not directly message staff members to argue a ruling. Use the proper Discord channels, follow the chain of command, or open a support ticket.

## 4. Lying to Staff

Do not lie to staff about anything related to New Republic RP. This includes in-game situations, Discord matters, player reports, refund requests, staff complaints, or any other community-related issue.

Do not mislead staff, falsify information, or submit edited or false evidence such as screenshots, videos, recordings, or logs.

## 5. Circumventing a Ban

If you are banned from the server or Discord, you may not use an alternate account or create a new account to bypass the ban.

Doing so may result in the alternate account being banned and the original ban being extended. Ban disputes must be handled through the proper Discord ticket process.

## 6. Game Modifications

Any modification that gives you an unfair advantage is prohibited.

This includes cheats, menus, scripts, plugins, crosshairs, vehicle handling modifications, performance-altering vehicle files, or any third-party tool that gives an advantage over other players.

Using unauthorized modifications may result in removal from the community.

## 7. Staff and Server Leadership Roles

Server leadership and department leadership must remain fair, professional, and free from conflicts of interest.

City council or oversight roles may not hold a department or group leader position, such as Chief, Sheriff, or similar leadership roles.

Administrators who also hold department or group leadership positions may not serve as the overall representative for that department group.

Staff members may not hold multiple server staff roles unless approved by leadership.

All staff members and department leaders are expected to keep sensitive server information confidential. This includes documents, player information, development plans, designs, internal discussions, and any other private community information.

## 8. VPN Access

VPN use is prohibited when joining the New Republic RP FiveM server.

If you have a special circumstance that requires VPN use, open a technical support ticket for review by Development and New Republic RP Leadership.

## 9. Harassment In or Out of the Community

Harassment will not be tolerated inside or outside of the community.

Do not harass, threaten, target, or intimidate someone because of something that happened in roleplay. Harassing someone for reporting a rule break is also strictly prohibited and may result in removal from the community.

## 10. No Voice Identification

Players may have multiple characters. Because of this, voice identification is not allowed.

You may not identify someone based only on their voice. This applies to law enforcement, EMS, fire, gangs, civilians, and all other players.
