# How to Join New Republic RP

Follow these steps to get started with New Republic RP.

## 1. Join the Discord

Join the New Republic RP Discord and review the announcement channels, rules, and application instructions.

## 2. Read the Guidebook

Before applying or entering the city, read the rules and roleplay expectations in this guidebook. Understanding the rules helps keep the server fair, realistic, and enjoyable for everyone.

## 3. Complete the Whitelist Application

Submit your whitelist application with accurate information. Staff may review your answers to confirm you understand the community standards and roleplay expectations.

## 4. Set Up FiveM

Make sure your FiveM client is updated and that your microphone works properly before joining the server.

## 5. Create Your Character

Build a realistic character with a believable name, background, goals, and personality. Your character should fit into the world and create roleplay for others.
