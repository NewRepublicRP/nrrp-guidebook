# Whitelist Application Guide

The whitelist application helps staff confirm that applicants understand New Republic RP rules, expectations, and roleplay standards.

## Application Tips

- Answer honestly.
- Use complete sentences.
- Explain your roleplay experience clearly.
- Show that you understand realistic roleplay.
- Do not copy answers from another applicant.

## What Staff Looks For

Staff looks for maturity, rule knowledge, creativity, and a willingness to contribute to the community in a positive way.
