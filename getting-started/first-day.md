# First Day in the City

Your first day should be focused on learning the city, meeting people, and building your character naturally.

## Recommended First Steps

1. Create a realistic character.
2. Learn basic controls and server commands.
3. Find legal work or civilian activities.
4. Meet other players in character.
5. Ask for help through in-character methods when possible.
6. Use Discord support only for technical issues or rule concerns.

## New Player Tips

Do not rush into criminal activity. Take time to understand the city, laws, departments, and economy. Strong roleplay starts with believable character development.
