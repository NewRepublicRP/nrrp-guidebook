# Law Enforcement

Law enforcement departments are responsible for public safety, traffic enforcement, investigations, warrants, arrests, and emergency response.

## Expectations

- Follow department SOPs.
- Use appropriate force.
- Respect suspect rights and due process in roleplay.
- Maintain professional radio traffic.
- Create quality roleplay for civilians and criminals.

## Specialty Units

Specialty units may include SWAT, traffic, K9, air support, investigations, motor units, marine units, crisis response, or other approved subdivisions.
